Communities

World-Historical Gazetteer (WHG) belongs to a growing community of project teams and individuals working to link data about the past, principally by linking data about historical places. We seek in turn to encourage communities of interest for particular regions, periods, and themes to establish specialized "domains" within WHG, linking data from their own data partners. This approach has worked so effectively for scholars of the Ancient Mediterranean and the Pleiades and Pelagios projects, it should stand as best practice.

Our first such domain is LatAm, which has been seeded by 14,150 records from Werner Stangl's HGIS de las Indias project. These include 13,258 lugares and 892 territorios covering Hispanic America from 1701-1808.
